Top 10 Universities in Tashkent

July 8, 2025

Tashkent, the capital city of Uzbekistan, is not only known for its rich history and culture but also for its rapidly growing education sector. In recent years, Tashkent has become a hub for students across Central Asia looking for quality higher education. Here are the top 10 universities in Tashkent you should know about:

1. Westminster International University in Tashkent (WIUT)

WIUT is one of the most prestigious universities in Uzbekistan, offering UK-style education in English. It is affiliated with the University of Westminster in London and offers undergraduate and postgraduate degrees in Business, Economics, IT, and Law.

2. Inha University in Tashkent

Inha University is a tech-focused institution, built in collaboration with South Korea. It specializes in Computer Science, Software Engineering, and ICT. The curriculum follows international standards, and graduates are highly employable in the tech sector.

3. Amity University Tashkent

As part of India’s global Amity network, this university brings high-quality programs in business, IT, and international relations. The modern campus and international faculty make it attractive for ambitious students.

4. Management Development Institute of Singapore (MDIS)

MDIS offers business and hospitality programs in partnership with UK universities. The campus in Tashkent has great facilities, and students have the option to complete their degree abroad.

5. Tashkent University of Information Technologies (TUIT)

TUIT is a national leader in telecommunications, cyber security, and software engineering. The university has a strong partnership with tech companies and government projects.

6. TEAM University

A modern business school with a startup and entrepreneurial focus. TEAM University promotes critical thinking, business innovation, and English-language education in business-related fields.

7. University of World Economy and Diplomacy (UWED)

UWED is a top government university that prepares students for careers in international relations, economics, and diplomacy. Many of Uzbekistan’s future diplomats and ministers are trained here.

8. Akfa University

Akfa is a private university backed by a large medical company. It offers programs in engineering, medicine, law, and digital technology. The campus is modern, and programs are in English.

9. Turin Polytechnic University in Tashkent

In cooperation with Politecnico di Torino (Italy), this university offers engineering and architecture degrees. Its programs are especially respected in mechanical and civil engineering.

10. Tashkent State University of Economics (TSUE)

TSUE is one of the oldest economic universities in Uzbekistan. It offers strong local and international programs in economics, finance, and management. The university is now expanding its English programs.

Conclusion

Whether you're looking for international education, tech, business, or diplomacy — Tashkent has something for you. Each university listed above offers unique advantages depending on your goals. Education in Uzbekistan is evolving fast — and now is the best time to be part of it!
