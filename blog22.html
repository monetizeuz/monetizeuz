Need Motivation to Study? Try These Techniques and Study Like a Lion!

July 8, 2025

Sometimes you just don’t feel like opening your book. Your brain is tired, your phone is tempting, and your future feels far away. But lions don’t quit — and neither should you. Here are quick motivation boosters to roar your way back into studying.

1. Remind Yourself WHY

Why are you studying? To change your life? To help your family? To make du'a come true? Write it down. Look at it daily.

2. Use the 5-Minute Rule

Tell yourself, “I’ll just study for 5 minutes.” Once you start, momentum builds. Most of the time, you’ll keep going.

3. Watch 1 Motivational Video

Pick 1 video that gets you hyped. Don’t scroll endlessly. Watch, feel the energy, and then immediately start your session.

4. Make Du’a Before Studying

Start with “Rabbi zidni ‘ilma.” Ask Allah for knowledge, focus, and discipline. Barakah flows when you connect first.

5. Change Your Environment

Messy desk = messy brain. Clean your space. Move to a new spot. Sit like a lion — not slouched like a sheep.

6. Use a Timer – Study Like a Warrior

Set a timer for 25–30 minutes. Go hard. Then rest for 5. Repeat. This is called the Pomodoro Technique — and it works.

7. Don’t Wait for Motivation — Build Discipline

Real students don’t rely on mood. They build systems. Set a time, show up daily. Even when it’s boring — especially then.

Conclusion

You’re not weak. You’re just waiting to remember your power. You were created to rise, not to scroll. Now get up, take a breath, say Bismillah, and start. Your future self is waiting.
